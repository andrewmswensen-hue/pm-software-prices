Submit a ticket. Questions? We’ve got answers. How is Rentvine priced? Rentvine uses a simple, all-inclusive pricing model based on the number of active units you manage, and you get access to every feature without extra fees or add-ons. Do I pay extra for core features like accounting, leasing, or portals? No. With Rentvine’s single pricing plan, core features like trust accounting, leasing automation, maintenance tools, and portals are all included with no feature paywalls. Are there any hidden fees or additional charges? No. The pricing page states there are no hidden fees, surprise charges, or upsells once you sign up. Is customer support included in the price? Yes. U.S.-based customer support is included in your subscription with average response times reported around 35 minutes. Do you charge per feature or per unit? Pricing is charged per active unit managed, not per feature, and you get every feature in the platform. Can I get a demo before I buy? Yes. You are encouraged to schedule a demo so you can see features and get pricing tailored to your portfolio size. Is onboarding included in the cost? Yes. Onboarding is included as part of your subscription, and most customers go live within 30–60 days with support for setup and data migration. Is there a minimum number of units I have to manage? Pricing does not require a minimum number of units, but pricing is based on your active unit count. Do you offer any promotions or discounts? At times, promotions are run such as offering months free when certain criteria are met, like enrolling in tenant screening and reaching a unit threshold. How do I get a personalized price estimate? You can contact for a custom quote based on your active units and specific business needs. Ready to deserve more?
