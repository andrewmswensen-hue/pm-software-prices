ShowMojo is an automated leasing platform that helps property managers fill vacancies at scale. From listing syndication and lead follow-up to showing coordination, smart device access, screening, and reporting, ShowMojo streamlines the leasing process so teams can scale operations, reduce vacancy, and grow without adding staff. How does ShowMojo work? When a prospect inquires about a rental — by phone, email, or text — ShowMojo responds automatically with customized messaging and presents available showing times based on your configured availability. The prospect self-screens by answering your qualification questions, selects a time, and receives automated confirmations and reminders. For self-guided tours, ShowMojo coordinates property access through integrated smart locks or lockboxes. After the showing, it follows up automatically to collect feedback. Your team is involved only when the situation calls for it. Who is ShowMojo designed for? ShowMojo is software built for the full range of residential property management: individual property managers managing single-family homes, growing property management companies, large portfolio operators with thousands of doors, and dedicated leasing shops. Both single-family and multifamily properties are supported. Plans scale with your portfolio — based on either average on-market listings (for smaller portfolios with faster turnover) or units under management (for larger portfolios). Does ShowMojo work in Canada? Yes, ShowMojo works in Canada and has served Canadian property managers since 2013. Nearly all of ShowMojo’s platform features are available to Canadian customers. Phone and text-based prospect communication features operate in both the U.S. and Canada.
