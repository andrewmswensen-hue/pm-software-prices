Toni Lewis You may also be wondering ... What is the Boom Platform? BoomReport? BoomScreen? The Boom Platform is Boom&#x27;s B2B platform where partners can access and manage their Boom products— BoomReport and BoomScreen . BoomReport is an automated rent reporting solution to help renters build credit with rent. BoomScreen is a configurable tenant screening tool to mitigate fraud and increase leasing velocity. Does Boom integrate with my property management software? Boom currently integrates with Buildium, Entrata, ManageAmerica, Quext, Rent Manager, Rentvine, ResMan, Yardi, and AppFolio (lite integration). If your PMS isn’t listed, reach out to the Boom team—we’re always working on adding new integrations. Additionally, any property manager can use a standard flat file (.csv) upload process for rent reporting that doesn’t require a PMS integration. Who do my renters and applicants reach out to for customer support? Renters and applicants can reach out to Boom&#x27;s customer support team via the Boom app (BoomReport), via their rental application (BoomScreen), or by emailing hello@boompay.app . The team is online 24/7 to help your renters and applicants with any issues or questions that may arise. Is there a pricing discount if I onboard with BoomScreen and BoomReport? Yes! Partners that utilize Boom for tenant screening and rent reporting will receive better rates for both products. Book a demo to learn more about pricing for your portfolio.
